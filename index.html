Our Vision and Mission

To declare Jesus Christ as our Lord and Savior,
and to spread the gospel.

Family
We welcome each family to join us, as we believe that the Lord is one family. We believe that the foundation of our church is in the love of God, and that we are committed to the love of our family.
Young Generation
See how we can help you grow in your faith and life. The new Generation is challenging and exciting. We want to help you grow by sharing the gospel.
Children
Our children are the first to experience the Lord's as they continue to grow in their faith. We guide them to the Lord's presence, and show them how to be a disciple, and how to be a leader.
Lifegroup
Life Group is a perfect place to be mentored by your peers. If you are stressed about an issue in your life, bring it to Life Group. Your Life Leader is there to help you through your struggles and pray alongside you. They also give great advice.
